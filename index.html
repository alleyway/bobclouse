Other bob facts

Bob formerly taught instrumental and vocal music in public schools and holds both B.S. and M.S. degrees in music education.

Bob really enjoys composing music, some of it ‘Serious’, most of it ‘Not Very’.

Bob is one of the ‘Grand Finalists’ in the search for a new Virginia State Song. His entry, “Oh, Virginia” can be heard on the ‘Original Compositions’ page of this Web site. . Bob hopes to live long enough to see a decision made by the selection committee. The contest started in 1998. The wheels turn slowly.

If you have extra time (a considerable amount) ask Bob about his seven grandkids.
